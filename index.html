Must-See Attractions

Baku Old City (Icherisheher)

A UNESCO World Heritage site featuring medieval architecture, the Maiden Tower, and the Palace of the Shirvanshahs.

Gobustan Rock Art

Ancient rock carvings and mud volcanoes dating back 40,000 years, showcasing prehistoric human life and artistic expression.

Flame Towers

Baku's iconic trio of skyscrapers, illuminated at night to resemble flames, symbolizing Azerbaijan's 'Land of Fire' heritage.

Sheki Historical Center

A UNESCO site featuring the Khan's Palace with stunning stained glass windows and traditional Azerbaijani architecture.

Caspian Sea Coast

Relax on the beautiful beaches and enjoy the unique Caspian coastline.

Lake Khanbulan

Discover the tranquil beauty of Lake Khanbulan, surrounded by lush forests and serene nature in southern Azerbaijan.
